Burger

Here's a great cheeseburger recipe

I hope you enjoy!

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef 80-20
  • Bread Buns
  • American Cheese
  • Tomatos
  • Mustard
  • Ketchup

Steps

  1. Season the ground beef or not. I use salt at least
  2. Mix the ground beef. And form patty shapes
  3. Prepare a cooking pan and place it on the stove
  4. Place the patties on the hot pan
  5. Sear the patties on both sides until cooked to your liking
  6. Prepare buns, stack the cooked burger meat with what ever you liked
